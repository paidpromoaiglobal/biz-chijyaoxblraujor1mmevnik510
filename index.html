About Us

ULTIMATE SPORTS

Ultimate Sports is OMR's most trusted sports establishment, located in the heart of Navalur on Rajiv Gandhi Salai, Chennai. Open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, we offer world-class badminton courts with professional-grade lighting, well-maintained surfaces, and a welcoming environment for players of all skill levels — from college students to seasoned athletes. Whether you're squeezing in an early morning session or a late-night game, Ultimate Sports is always ready for you.

With a rating of 4.2/5 from over 391 reviews, our facility is recognized as one of the best badminton venues in the OMR corridor. Our new management team has brought fresh energy, a highly supportive coaching staff, and a commitment to excellent customer service. We take pride in creating a comfortable, organized, and inclusive sporting experience that keeps our members coming back year after year.

  • Open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — play on your schedule
  • Best badminton courts in the OMR area with superior lighting and clean surfaces
  • Friendly, cooperative staff and professional coaching team
